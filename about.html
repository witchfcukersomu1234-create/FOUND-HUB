Innovating for a Smarter Campus

Founded by a dedicated team of second-year IT students, Found-Hub is a platform built by students, for students. Our vision was born out of a common campus challenge: the frustration of lost gadgets and the difficulty in returning found items.

We believe in leveraging technology to create practical solutions that enhance daily campus life. Found-Hub is more than just a system; it's a community initiative designed to foster responsibility, trust, and connection among college principles, staff, and students. We are forward-thinking, providing an innovative solution for everyone.
